Our Services

Custom Jewelry

Our team specializes in designing and crafting complex
custom jewelry in gold or platinum. Our 3D designers can
work with even the most complex ideas and create a digital
model for you to approve before committing to production.

Engagement Ring

Our team of 3D modelers and jewelers can create literally any
ring you can imagine, including complex designs. We're opening
up our services to private clients, so you can get a beautiful,
designer quality engagement ring at an affordable price.

Jewelry Manufacturing

We work with you to make sure your jewelry is produced

can manufacture a unique collection for your store, or even
work with custom orders for your customers.
